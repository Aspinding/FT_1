Every Brella plan comes with a benefit for Moderate, Severe, and Catastrophic conditions. Employees can select the payout amounts that fit their needs and their budget.

Employers can fund part or all of the premiums—or offer Brella as a voluntary benefit. The choice is up to you.

We built Brella to make your life easier.

Brella is 100% paperless from quote to contract to claim. Plus, our online Employer Portal and flexible enrollment options make administration a snap.

Employees can use our online shopping platform to learn about Brella and customize their benefits.

Enroll via our platform or we’ll plug into yours. We work with most major benefits administration platforms.

Forget forms and faxes. Employees can file a claim via our mobile app or online member portal.

They’ll answer a few questions and upload photos of documents they have on hand from their provider visit. That’s it!

With Brella, your people can file a claim as soon as they’re diagnosed with information they may have on hand.

We pay benefits on approved claims via Venmo, Paypal, or to a designated bank account— typically within 72 hours. Employees can choose their payment preferences in advance.

Both employers and employees have dedicated support throughout their membership. So you can talk to the same person any time you call.

Our team is standing by to listen and answer questions Monday through Friday.
